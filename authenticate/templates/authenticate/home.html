About

Welcome to one of the largest students association of RUET combining the students from Jessore,Ghenaidah,Magura and Narail.The idea behind this association is to form an strong understanding between RUETians of same culture and help each other to survive and complete one of the important chapter in our life.Here we take advices from our seniors about career and also academic needs. We organize different types of seminars and cultural events to celebrate our RUET life together and share our life experiences. We also have an enreached library from where students can collect their academic books for free. The idea behind this website is to make everyone one step closer to each other and share our experiences and important informations.
